## Retention Strategy Recommendations

Based on the churn analysis and machine learning model results, the following business actions are recommended:

### 1. Promote Long-Term Contracts

Customers on month-to-month contracts showed the highest churn rates. Offering discounts and incentives for one-year or two-year contracts can improve customer retention.

### 2. Target High-Risk Customers Early

Customers with lower tenure were more likely to churn. Retention campaigns should focus on customers within their first 18 months of service.

### 3. Reduce Pricing-Related Churn

Customers with higher monthly charges demonstrated increased churn risk. Personalized discounts and bundled service plans can help reduce cancellations.

### 4. Improve Customer Support Services

Features such as Tech Support and Online Security were identified as important churn factors. Improving support quality and promoting value-added services can increase customer satisfaction.

### 5. Implement Predictive Retention Programs

The churn prediction model can be used to identify customers at risk of leaving, enabling proactive retention campaigns before churn occurs.
